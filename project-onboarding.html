Onboarding

New Hire Onboarding Program

New team members need a structured way to understand their role, tools, documentation, stakeholders, and performance expectations without being overwhelmed.

AudienceNew hires, program support staff, managers, and team leads
RoleInstructional Designer, Learning Program Coordinator
DeliveryILT, hybrid, webinar, and self-paced support options

Design Approach

The design sequences learning over the first 90 days, moving from orientation to guided practice to independent contribution. The roadmap pairs manager check-ins with checklists and resource hub navigation so new hires know what to learn, where to find help, and how readiness is measured.

Reflection

This project demonstrates how instructional design can reduce ramp-up time and create consistency across teams.
