AI Training

AI Literacy for Workplace Productivity

Adult learners need practical, beginner-friendly AI training that explains core concepts, safe use, and prompt writing in a way that connects to real workplace tasks.

AudienceAdult workforce learners with mixed technical experience
RoleInstructional Designer, Facilitator, AI Course Developer
DeliveryILT, hybrid, webinar, and self-paced support options

Design Approach

The design begins with common workplace tasks such as drafting, summarizing, planning, and reviewing content. Learners build from foundational AI concepts to hands-on prompt writing and responsible use decisions. Practice is supported through worksheets, examples, knowledge checks, and a capstone workplace application activity.

Reflection

This project shows how complex emerging technology can be translated into clear, confidence-building learning for non-technical audiences.

Case Study Summary
