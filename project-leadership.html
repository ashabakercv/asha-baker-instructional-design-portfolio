Leadership Development

Leadership Communication Microlearning

New managers need a short, practical learning experience that helps them practice giving feedback that is specific, respectful, and actionable.

AudienceNew managers, team leads, and emerging leaders
RoleInstructional Designer, Storyboard Developer
DeliveryILT, hybrid, webinar, and self-paced support options

Design Approach

The microlearning design uses a realistic scenario, branching choices, feedback, and a simple model learners can use during real conversations. The module is short enough for busy managers but includes a downloadable job aid to support behavior after the lesson.

Skills Demonstrated

MicrolearningStoryboardingScenario DesignBranchingLeadership TrainingReflection

Reflection

This project demonstrates behavior-based design for soft skills training, where learners practice decisions and receive feedback rather than simply reading advice.

Case Study Summary

  • Learning problem defined
  • Audience needs considered
  • Practice and performance support included
  • Assessment or evaluation included
  • Samples are original and sanitized
